The country of Canada is 9.98 million square kilometers long and touches both Pacific and Atlantic Ocean! Across this giant land, Canada is split into Provinces and Territories. There are 10 Provinces and 3 Territories. Within each Province and Territory are the cities and towns. The biggest cities in Canada are Toronto in the Province of Ontario, Montreal, in Quebec, and Vancouver in British Columbia.

Fun Facts about Canada's Provinces and Territories

British Columbia - Province

This province is found on the very west side of the country!

Alberta - Province

Alberta is home to A LOT of dinosaur bones!

Saskatchewan - Province

Sunny Saskatchewan gets the most hours of sunshine than any other province in Canada!

Manitoba - Province

One of Manitoba's towns, Churchill, is known as the "Polar Bear Capital of the World"!

Ontario - Province

Home to the biggest city in all of Canada! Toronto!

Quebec - Province

Canada's french-speaking province and home to half a million lakes!

New Brunswick - Province

New Brunswick is home to the the highest tides in the Bay of Fundy!

Nova Scotia - Province

Nova Scotia is almost completely surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean!

Prince Edward Island - Province

Canada's smallest province!

Newfoundland & Labrador - Province

The city of St.John's is famously known for bright and colourful homes!

Yukon - Territory

The longest river in Canada can be found in the Yukon!

Northwest Territories - Territory

Endless sunshine, known as "Midnight Sun" begins in May until the end of July

Nunavut - Territory

Home to the Haughton Crater which some say is the closest you can come to what Mars is like!
