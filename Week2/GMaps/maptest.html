Snow, Snow, and more Snow...

The mountains of British Columbia couples with a weather pattern that creates endless centomemtres of snow, British Coumbia is a skier and snowboarder's paradice. From a simple 30 minute drive out of downtown Vancouver that take you to the local northshore mountain resorts of Cypress, Seymour, and Grouse mountain, to the most eastern hill of endless powder days at Fernie Alpine Resort, the choices are endless.
