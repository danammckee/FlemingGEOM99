Snow, Snow, and more Snow...

The mountains of British Columbia couples with a weather pattern that creates endless centomemtres of snow, British Coumbia is a skier and snowboarder's paradice. From a simple 30 minute drive out of downtown Vancouver that take you to the local northshore mountain resorts of Cypress, Seymour, and Grouse mountain, to the most eastern hill of endless powder days at Fernie Alpine Resort, the choices are endless.

Planning Your Trip

When visiting British Columbia for an amazing ski/snowboard vacation, the endless choices may be a bit much. Start by accessing what type of adventure you want based on location. Is ease of travel your priority? Stick to resorts with major airports near them such as the local Vancouver mountains and Whistler, or even head a bit fruther to Big White Ski Resort that has the Kelwona airport near by. With more planning, you can head deeper into the Province and seek out Resorts with the steepest steeps such as Kicking Horse Mountain by Golden, BC. Happy shredding!
