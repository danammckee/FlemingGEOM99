Stave Lake

Stave Lake is a popular recreational site in Mission, BC for camping, fishing, boating, hiking, ATV riding, mountain biking, picnicking, swimming and more. In addition to being a popular recreational areas for the publish, Stave Lake is also a reservoir for the production of hydroelectricity in the Stave River system. Stave Lake is located approximately 65 kilometers from Vancouver, BC. The long lake runs north to south and is 20 kilometers at it's longest arm and is 55 square kilometers. The lake meets Stave Falls Dame. After construction of the dam, the lake increased in size by about one-third its current size.
